title: Irssi: simple alias to recover nickname date: 2008-05-08 23:48:01

Simple alias to recover your nick on freenode with Irssi:

/alias nick_recover msg NickServ RECOVER $0 $1; wait 2000; msg NickServ RELEASE $0 $1; wait 2000; nick henry; wait 2000; msg NickServ IDENTIFY $1

To use it simply type: 

/nick_recover nickname password

Update: Freenode just replaced the recover command by ghost, so now use:

/alias nick_recover msg NickServ GHOST $0 $1; wait 2000; msg NickServ RELEASE $0 $1; wait 2000; nick henry; wait 2000; msg NickServ IDENTIFY $1
