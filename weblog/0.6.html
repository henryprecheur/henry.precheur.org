title: Weblog 0.6 date: 2008-2-26

Weblog 0.6 is out. This release allows you to use relative URI. They will be rewritten to make them point to the right URI.

Example: <a href='test.html'> will turn into <a href='http://my.blog.org/test.html'> when needed. Not exactly a killer feature, but it is really convenient!

As usual go check Weblog's page

Or download Weblog-0.6 directly.
A standalone version of Weblog including Jinja is also available: Weblog + Jinja.
